**Get your candidates AIP-ready — in Arabic and English — and see exactly who's there.**

## The problem

AIP / AIS knowledge is examinable across pilot licensing, licence conversion, and AIS/ATS/dispatch
roles — but most organisations still train it from scattered PDFs, in English only, with no way to
tell who is actually ready to sit.

## What you get

- **A structured AIP prep pack**, bilingual (EN + Arabic, right-to-left), on web and mobile:
  what the AIP is (ICAO Annex 15), the AIRAC cycle, and the full GEN / ENR / AD structure of the
  14 Saudi eAIP documents.
- **A cited question bank + Mock Exam.** Every question explains the answer and links to the
  Part/section, so candidates learn to verify — not memorise.
- **Captain Adel**, an AI study assistant that answers "where in the AIP does it say…" with a
  citation, in either language.
- **An admin dashboard.** Invite your cohort by email or CSV, watch coverage and Mock Exam
  scores, and export a **readiness report** for each candidate's training file.

## Why Fly GACA

- **Saudi-specific and bilingual.** Built on the Saudi eAIP and GACAR corpus, Arabic-first RTL —
  not a generic international course.
- **Citation-backed.** Nothing is hand-waved; it all points back to the source of truth.
- **Readiness you can report.** A "ready to sit" flag per candidate, exportable for your records.
- **No build cost to you.** The content is live today; you get seats, an admin, and a report.

## How it works

1. We set up your cohort and (Academy+) a co-branded landing with your logo.
2. Candidates work the study sheet → reading → question bank → Mock Exam loop (~6 hours of
   content, typically over 2–3 weeks).
3. You watch progress and export the readiness report when they hit your threshold.
