SUBPART A-GENERAL

§ 29.A Standards Incorporated by Reference.

(a) Subject to the exceptions noted in Subpart B of this part, the airworthiness standards for transport category rotorcraft are as specified in Part 29 of Title 14, Code of Federal Regulations (14 CFR) of the United States of America, Airworthiness Standards: Transport Category Rotorcraft, as amended.

(b) The adoption by reference of 14 CFR Part 29 includes previous amended versions of the regulation as cited in the GACAR.

Example:

A reference to GACAR § 29.1 effective 12 March, 1980 is equivalent to 14 CFR § 29.1 effective 12 March, 1980.

GACAR PART 29 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: TRANSPORT CATEGORY ROTORCRAFT
