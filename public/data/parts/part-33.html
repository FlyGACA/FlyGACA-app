SUBPART A -GENERAL

§ 33.A Standards Incorporated by Reference.

(a) Subject to the exceptions noted in Subpart B of this part, the airworthiness standards for aircraft engines are as specified in Part 33 of Title 14, Code of Federal Regulations (14 CFR) of the United States of America, Airworthiness Standards: Aircraft Engines, as amended.

(b) The adoption by reference of 14 CFR Part 33 includes previous amended versions of the regulation as cited in the GACAR.

Example:

A reference to GACAR § 33.1 effective 12 March, 1980 is equivalent to 14 CFR § 33.1 effective 12 March, 1980.
