# Part 121 — Operating Requirements: Domestic, Flag, and Supplemental Operations

> Educational summary. Fly GACA is **not affiliated with GACA**; always verify against the
> official GACAR before operating.

## Subpart A — General

This Part prescribes rules governing the operations of each holder of an air-operator certificate
issued under Part 119. Except where this Part imposes a more specific requirement, the general
operating and flight rules of [Part 91](./part-91.md) continue to apply to every flight.

Aircraft maintenance must be performed under a program acceptable per Part 43, and crewmember
certification follows Part 61.

## Subpart T — Flight Operations

No certificate holder may dispatch a flight unless the dispatch release meets § 121.687. Minimum
equipment requirements cross-reference § 91.205 for required instruments and equipment.
